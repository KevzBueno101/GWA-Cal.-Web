Grading System and GWA in the Philippines

Overview of Grading in the Philippines

In most Philippine schools and universities, student performance is measured using a numerical grading system on a scale of 1 to 5, 60 to 100, or percentage equivalents, depending on the institution. Grades reflect how well a student has achieved learning outcomes in each subject.

General Weighted Average (GWA)

The General Weighted Average (GWA) is used to summarize a student’s overall academic performance across all subjects. It is calculated as follows:

GWA = (Σ (Grade × Units)) ÷ (Σ Units)

Where:

  • Grade = numeric score of each subject
  • Units = credit units for each subject

Academic Honors: Dean's List & President's List

Many Philippine universities recognize outstanding student performance through honors such as the Dean's List and President's List:

  • Dean's List: Students with a General Weighted Average (GWA) typically between 1.00 and 1.75 (on a 1.00–5.00 scale) with no failing grades may be included on the Dean's List. This distinction is awarded per semester.
  • President's List: This is usually the highest honor, often awarded to students with a perfect or near-perfect GWA (e.g., 1.00) and exemplary academic performance.

These honors encourage academic excellence and are recognized across most Philippine higher education institutions.

About GWA Calculator App

What This App Is

GWA Calculator is a lightweight Windows tool that helps students compute their General Weighted Average instantly. It removes the hassle of manual computation and makes grade checking faster, easier, and more accurate.

Why It Was Created

I built this app after struggling with manual grade computation—double-checking formulas, multiplying units and grades, and computing everything one by one. I wanted a simple tool that could compute GWA instantly without errors.

Core Features

  • Instant and accurate GWA computation
  • Beginner-friendly and clean UI
  • Works fully offline
  • No data collection
  • Lightweight and fast

Who This App Helps

Designed for students who want an easier, error-free way to compute their academic standing or check if they qualify for honors.

Privacy & Safety

Your data stays on your device — the app never connects to any external servers and works entirely offline.
